Overview

Purpose

The purpose of this website is to be informative, help you learn about White Water Rafting, and help you come to choose our venue.

Audience

This is a site that is for families, friends, school or church groups. They can come here to enjoy the outdoors and learn about white water rafting. We will have package deals on gear for families and groups.
